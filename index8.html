    If you can dream it, you can build it with the 'Minecraft' Xbox 360 Edition game. This fantasy game allows gamers to create worlds using their imaginations. Play as a single player or let friends join in on the building fun. With Xbox LIVE, up to eight people can play together. In Minecraft, players craft everything you can think of, from pets to castles. The sky is the limit.
